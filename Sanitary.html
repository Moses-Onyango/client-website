    • Menstrual Hygiene Challenges in Kibera

    Menstrual hygiene remains a significant challenge for many women and girls in Kibera.
    The high cost of sanitary pads, coupled with limited access to clean water and sanitation
    facilities, often forces women and girls to resort to unhygienic alternatives, such as using
    old rags or even leaves. This can lead to a range of health problems, including infections,
    reproductive tract diseases, and even social isolation.

    Recognizing the urgency of addressing these issues, Tembea Kibera embarked on a mission
    to provide free sanitary pads to girls and women in Kibera. The organization's sanitary pad
    giveaways not only ensure that women and girls have access to essential menstrual products
    but also serve as an educational platform, dispelling myths and taboos surrounding menstruation
    and promoting healthy menstrual hygiene practices.

    Impact of Sanitary Pad Giveaways

    Tembea Kibera's sanitary pad giveaways have had a profound impact on the lives of girls and women in Kibera. By providing access to essential menstrual products, the organization has empowered women and girls to manage their menstruation with dignity and confidence. This has led to improved attendance rates in schools, increased participation in community activities, and a greater sense of well-being among women and girls.

    Tembea Kibera's sanitary pad giveaways serve as a beacon of hope for women and girls in Kibera, addressing a critical need and promoting their health, education, and overall well-being. The organization's dedication to menstrual hygiene education and empowerment is a testament to its unwavering commitment to creating a more equitable and just society for all.
