    • Our Operations

    Tembea Kibera is dedicated to making a lasting and positive impact
    on the lives of Kibera residents through an array of community outreach
    programs and community investment initiatives. Our mission goes
    beyond merely acknowledging the challenges faced by the residents;
    we are committed to fostering positive change and empowerment
    within the community.

    One of our flagship initiatives is the Slum Tour, a unique experience
    designed not only to showcase the vibrant aspects of our community
    but also to provide participants with insights into the transformative
    projects that we undertake. The tour includes regular stops at
    landmarks such as our office, the Women's Empowerment Project,
    Kibera Town Center, the Victoria Bone Group, and various locally-owned
    businesses. Through these stops, participants gain a comprehensive
    understanding of the dynamic and progressive facets of Kibera.

    At the heart of our mission lies a commitment to positively transforming
    the lives of the vibrant community of Kibera through a multifaceted
    approach encompassing community outreach programs and strategic
    community investment initiatives. Our dedication to this cause is manifested
    in our impactful Slum Tour, a unique experience that transcends conventional
    tourism. During this tour, we go beyond merely showcasing Kibera's
    socio-economic landscape; we delve into the community's most positive and
    progressive facets.
